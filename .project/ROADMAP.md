## In Progress

### Footnote/Endnote Support
**Branch**: `feat/footnote-support`
**Why**: Academic and legal documents frequently use footnotes. Currently unsupported
in the markdown-to-docx pipeline.
**Scope**: Parse markdown footnote syntax → generate Word footnotes with proper styling.
